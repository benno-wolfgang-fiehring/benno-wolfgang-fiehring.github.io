  • Bachelor’s Degree in Archaeology

    Degree Awarded: Licenciado en Arqueología (2024)

    Duration: 9 semesters (full-time program)

    Escuela Nacional de Antropología e Historia (ENAH) (2011-2015)

    Mexico City, Mexico

    Supervisor: Arqlgo. Luis Antonio Huitrón Santoyo

    Advisors: Dr. Lidia Iris Rodríguez Rodríguez and Dr. Omar Olivo del Olmo

    Thesis: "Custodians of the Mountain: The Principles of Mazatec Communality and Spirituality as Mechanisms for the Preservation of Cultural Heritage”

  • Diploma in Architecture (equivalent to Master’s degree)

    Degree Awarded: Diplom-Ingenieur (Dipl.-Ing.) (2010)

    Duration: 10 semesters (full-time program)

    Technische Universität Berlin (2004-2010)

    Berlin, Germany

    First Examiner: Prof. Dr. Peter Herrle, Habitat Unit, TU Berlin

    Second Examiner: Prof. Dr.-Ing. Johannes Cramer, Architectural and Urban History, TU Berlin

    Thesis: “Potentials of Historical Architecture in the City Centre of Mexico City”
