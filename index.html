Hello! My name is Benno Wolfgang Fiehring, and this is my personal website. I’d like to share it with you so you can get to know me a bit better.

I studied architecture in Berlin, Germany, and during my studies I began working in Mexico. I was captivated by Mexico’s colors, its landscapes, and its people. I was particularly drawn to its pre-Hispanic past, which led me to study archaeology and dedicate myself to the conservation of Mexico’s cultural heritage.

Today, I believe that heritage conservation only has value if it serves society in the present and for the future. In a country marked by deep inequalities, this makes our work all the more compelling!
